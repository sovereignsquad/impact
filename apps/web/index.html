What you get in under a minute

  • Local inventory — what is installed, reachable, and how confidently we know it.
  • Readable report — open impact-report.html in a browser after one command.
  • Optional community loop — submit an anonymous payload if you choose; aggregates on this site will appear only when ingest and privacy thresholds are met (Community data).

Not shipped: benchmark leaderboards, DMG/app installer, or “AI readiness” scores — see current-state.md.

FAQ

Does IMPACT upload my data?

No by default. Scans stay on your machine unless you opt into submission. See privacy for users.

Is this a benchmark?

No. v0.x is honest discovery and export. Benchmarks are programme roadmap, not current product claims.
