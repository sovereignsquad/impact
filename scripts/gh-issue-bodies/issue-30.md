## Objective

Preserve the broader long-range direction for anonymous submission, ingest, storage, aggregation, and governance as a coherent community-data system.

## Unified Context

IMPACT now has:
- a working scanner
- a public web shell
- an activation path for hosted ingest and stats
- a live same-origin `/api` stats path on the web layer
- ongoing work to turn repo-complete backend pieces into product-live infrastructure

This roadmap card is **not** the active execution spine for that work.

Instead, it exists to preserve the broader future direction for:
- submission system maturity
- ingest hardening
- storage evolution
- aggregation architecture
- governance and publication safety

## Theme / Goal

Preserve the future direction for:
- anonymous submission flow
- ingest service hardening
- durable storage
- aggregation evolution
- governance, abuse controls, and publication rules

The goal is to ensure community visibility matures into a governed system, not just a tactical activation path.

## Why this matters

Without this roadmap theme:
- ingest may remain tactical instead of evolving into an operational service
- governance may lag behind public visibility
- storage and aggregation may drift without a longer horizon
- public data features may outrun privacy and abuse controls

## What this does not mean yet

This issue does **not** mean:
- re-planning the current dashboard activation tranche
- building a heavy data platform now
- adding accounts, leaderboards, or benchmark overlays now
- replacing the current activation order with speculative platform design

Governance and anti-abuse are **later** programme concerns, not current blockers on the activation path.

This card exists to preserve the future service direction, not to override the current delivery spine.

## Risks of misunderstanding

The main risk is confusing a roadmap theme with execution authority.

If misunderstood, the team may:
- over-engineer the backend before the first live dashboard proves value
- merge governance theory with immediate ops work
- create platform churn instead of finishing the activation path
